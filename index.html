FINANCIAL FRAUD AND IDENTITY

THEFT IN A DIGITAL AGE

Analytical Report

The development of online banking, debit, and credit cards has completely changed the way ofaccessing money. People are using debit and credit cards instead of cash because it is more convenient than carrying money. Many businesses around the world rely on technology to reach their customer in an efficient and secure way. Unfortunately, debit and credit card transactions are not as secure as one might hope. Cyber hackers are using viruses, spam, and spywares to hack the system of banking and businesses companies to defraud money and the valuable information of the customer. This report describes how financial fraud and identity theft is a serious growing issue in today’s economy and how it affects the entire world.

AVERTING FINANCIAL FRAUD

IDENTITY THEFT

Proposal of solution

The growing financial fraud and identity theft can only be solved by the combined efforts of government, business and finance companies, and people. Strong laws and financial penalties need to be introduced from the government side against the cyber criminals to discourage them. From the business side, financial institutions and business companies need to implement a cyber-security system to mitigate the possible attacks from cyber hackers and cyber criminals. Furthermore, people need to install antivirus programs to their computers and need to have caution while using the internet to buy things from the business companies. Finally, people must keep track of their monetary transactions regularly and should implement security alert systems on their phones that are offered by the bank and finance industry.
